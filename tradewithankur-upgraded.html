FAQ

Common Questions

What is ICT trading and why should I learn it?+
ICT (Inner Circle Trader) is a methodology developed by Michael J. Huddleston that teaches traders to think like institutions — understanding how smart money (banks, hedge funds) manipulate price to hunt retail stop losses before moving in their real direction. Learning ICT gives you a genuine edge in any market.
Is this course for complete beginners?+
Yes! The Basic Foundation course starts from absolute zero — what is a stock, how to open a Demat account, what is a candlestick. Even if you've never traded before, you can start here and progress to the Pro and Elite programs.
Will I get SEBI-registered tips or signals?+
No — and that's intentional. This is pure education, not a tip service. We do NOT provide buy/sell signals, recommendations, or stock tips. We teach you HOW to read the market yourself. Anyone selling signals is not doing you a favour in the long run.
Do I get lifetime access to the videos?+
Yes. Once you enroll, you get lifetime access to all course content plus all future updates to that course. You can watch at your own pace, rewatch any video anytime, and access from mobile or desktop.
What is the refund policy?+
We offer a 7-day refund window if you've watched less than 20% of the course and feel it's not the right fit. After 7 days or after completing 20%+ content, no refunds are provided. Please see our full Refund Policy page for details.
How much capital do I need to start trading?+
For paper trading (practice) — zero. For real trading, we recommend starting with at least ₹50,000 for equity and ₹1,00,000 for F&O. But more importantly, never trade money you can't afford to lose while you're learning.
Is there any community or support?+
Yes! All students get access to our private Telegram community where you can post charts, ask questions, and get feedback. Pro and Elite students also get weekly live Q&A sessions with Ankur directly.
